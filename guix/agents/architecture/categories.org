** No external channels
Never add external channels. Package definitions go directly into submodules.

** Kernel Space

kernel-space/linux.scm exports:
- linux (from nonguix)

kernel-space/kernel-space.scm aggregates:
- bootloader, filesystem, hostname, keyboard, locale, elogind, udev, kmod
