:CATEGORY: ai
:VERSION:  0.1.0

** Overview
:PROPERTIES:
:tangle: no
:END:

MCP (Model Context Protocol) enables Emacs to communicate with AI models and LLMs through a standardized protocol. Perfect for integrating OpenCode and other AI assistants directly into your editor.

*Key Features:*
- Standard protocol for AI communication
- Works with Claude, OpenAI, and other models
- Share context from your buffer with AI
- Insert AI responses directly into documents
- Error handling and streaming support
