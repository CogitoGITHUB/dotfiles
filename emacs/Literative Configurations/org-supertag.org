* Org-Supertag
:PROPERTIES:
:DISABLED: t
:END:

Hierarchical tagging system for Org-mode with powerful query and filtering.

WARNING: org-supertag requires many dependencies (gptel, posframe, and many internal modules).
It's complex to integrate automatically. To use:

#+BEGIN_SRC elisp
M-x eval-expression
(progn
  (add-to-list 'load-path "~/.config/emacs/lisp/org-supertag")
  (require 'org-supertag)
  (org-supertag-mode 1))
#+END_SRC

Source: https://github.com/yibie/org-supertag.git
