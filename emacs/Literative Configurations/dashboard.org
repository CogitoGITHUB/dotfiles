* Dashboard
:PROPERTIES:
:PACKAGE: dashboard
:END:

Startup screen showing recent files, bookmarks, and system status.

#+BEGIN_SRC elisp
(leaf dashboard
  :when (>= emacs-major-version 27)
  :require t
  :custom
  ((dashboard-center-content . t)
   (dashboard-startup-banner . 'official)
   (dashboard-items . '((recents   . 5)
                        (bookmarks . 5)
                        (projects  . 5))))
  :hook (after-init-hook . dashboard-open))
#+END_SRC
